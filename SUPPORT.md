# Support

Use the issue tracker in the relevant public repository for a problem with its
documents, assets, or other published materials. Include a link or file path,
what you expected, and what should be corrected.

For help with the Mint Shelf marketplace, an account, or an order, visit the
[Help Center](https://mintshelf.com/help) or email
[hello@mintshelf.com](mailto:hello@mintshelf.com).

Do not include vulnerability details in a public issue. Follow the private
reporting instructions in the [security policy](SECURITY.md).
